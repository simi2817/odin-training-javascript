Welcome to the Monopoly World!

Monopoly is derived from The Landlord's Game , created by Lizzie Magie in the United States in 1903 as a way to demonstrate that an economy that rewards individuals is better than one where monopolies hold all the wealth. The game is named after the economic concept of a monopoly—the domination of a market by a single entity.
