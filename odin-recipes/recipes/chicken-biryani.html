Chicken Biryani

Biryani is mixed rice dish made with spices, vegetables and usually some type of meat (chicken). Biryani is usually served with raita, pickle and salad.

Ingredients

  • 300g basmati rice
  • 25g butter
  • 1 large onion, finely sliced
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 3 cardamom pods
  • small cinnamon stick
  • 1 tsp tumeric
  • 4 chicken breasts, cut into chunks
  • 85g raisins
  • 850ml chicken stock
  • 30g coriander
  • 4 tbsp balti curry paste

Steps

  1. Soak basmati rice in warm water and wash until water runs clear.
  2. Heat butter in a pan and cook onion with bay leaf, cardomonm and cinnamon for 10 min.
  3. Sprinkle tumeric, add 4 chicken breasts and curry paste.
  4. Stir the rice into pan with raisins, and pour chicken stock.
  5. Place a lid and bring to hard boil, lower heat to minimum and cook 5 mins.
  6. Turn off the heat and leave for 10 mins. Stir well, mixing coriander.
